About The Band

KENOFISH are currently a two-piece alternative/indie rock band from just outside of London, consisting of founding members Matthew Sandfort (Guitar, Vocals) and James Ball (Drums).

Matthew Sandfort

Taking inspiration from all kinds of music genres, KENOFISH are rooted within that 90s rock feel, with a current vibe.

As it stands, the band is looking for two new members to join. A bass player and a guitarist to complete the full sound. If interested please contact us via our email or social media.
